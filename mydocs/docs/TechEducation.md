### Circuit Parasitics

The success of physical circuit design is influenced by the physical reality of component tolerances, and parasitics like electromagnetic interference generated by component or circuits unintentionally affecting the operation of other components or circuits. This can happen through radiation radio waves through air or conduction through wires or circut traces. These effects are usually difficult to measure and correct.

### Software

I was drawn to software Engineering because of the difficlties  correcting hardware parasitics. Support software, hardware or network errors can usually be traced and corrected.

### Senior Project

The final year of Electronic Engineering classes included designing, developing, and documenting a Senior Project before graduation.

I developed a software program after learning some aspects of the Fortran programming language. The progrm utilized the Fourier transform, a mathematical technique that converts a repetitive waveform from the time domain to the frequency domain. This process reveals the frequencies present in a repetitive waveform and their respective amplitudes. The software underwent laboratory testing using a waveform generator and a spectrum analyzer instrument. The spectrum analyzer visualizes and measures electronic signals based on their frequency content.
