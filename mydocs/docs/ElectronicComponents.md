# Electronic Components

The journey began with receiving an Allied Electronics catalog, sparking interest in both familiar and new components.

## Learning Resources

I expanded my knowledge through subscriptions to weekly and monthly electronics periodicals, which introduced new components and equipment and provided application insights.

Bookstores, both local and in other cities, served as important sources of new books for my growing technical library, although some books were occasionally outdated.

The Internet and Artificial Intelligence are transformative for technology learning, replacing the manual collection and analysis of information from varied sources.

Utilizing an RSS (Really Simple Syndication) Internet application daily enables track technologies of interest.
